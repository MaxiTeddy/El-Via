    WHAT TO EXCEPT

    *If a customer does business with us as a real estate company, here's what they can expect regarding our services*

    LAND:

    We provide detailed information about the land, including its size, boundaries, topography, and any potential restrictions or limitations. We conduct thorough research to ensure that the land is suitable for your intended use and meets your requirements.

    SURROUNDINGS:

    We provide information about the neighborhood, including amenities, schools, parks, transportation options, and nearby attractions. We consider factors such as safety, convenience, and potential for future growth or development, ensuring that the surroundings align with your lifestyle preferences.

    MAINTENANCE:

    We provide guidance and information about the maintenance requirements of the property. This includes information about landscaping, infrastructure, utilities, and any ongoing costs associated with the property. We also help you connect with trusted contractors or service providers who can assist with property maintenance and repairs.

    DUE DILIGENCE:

    As part of our services, we conduct thorough due diligence on the property and its surroundings. This includes checking for any potential environmental concerns, zoning regulations, land use restrictions, or future development plans that may affect the property.

    PROPERTY MANAGEMENT:

    If you require assistance with property management, we can provide you with recommendations for reputable property management companies. These companies can handle tasks such as rent collection, tenant placement, maintenance, and accounting, allowing you to enjoy a hassle-free ownership experience

    HOMEOWNER ASSOCIATIONS:

    If the property is located within a homeowner association (HOA), we will provide you with all the relevant information about the association, its rules, regulations, and any associated fees. We will ensure that you have a clear understanding of the HOA's policies before making a decision.

    EXPERT GUIDANCE

    Our real estate agents have extensive knowledge and expertise in the industry. They will guide you through the entire process, from property search to closing the deal. You can expect valuable advice and insights to help you make informed decisions.
